Job specification

We are looking for web designers who will be responsible for creating great websites for our clients. For this position you will need to be good at coming up with brilliant ideas for the clients websites and creating visual elements that are with the clients expectations. If you want to work with Hot Beans then you will need to have certain qualifications and have a good general knowledge with computers.
